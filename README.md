# Your AI Study Partner

> StudyLog.ai — AI study companion. Flashcards, spaced repetition, concept mapping. Part of the Lucineer ecosystem.

## What It Does

- Smart flashcards with spaced repetition
- Concept mapping and knowledge graphs
- Practice quizzes from your notes
- Study session planning and focus timers
- Progress tracking and weak-spot identification
- Multi-subject support with cross-referencing

## How It Works

studylog-ai is a themed Cocapn instance. Behind the scenes:

- **Equipment** — What it perceives: your notes, documents, inputs
- **Skills** — How it thinks: domain expertise, reasoning patterns
- **Context** — What it remembers: conversation history, progress

## The Ecosystem

- **Layer 1 — Touch:** studylog-ai, studylog.ai, activelog.ai, dmlog.ai — "It just works."
- **Layer 2 — Operate:** cocapn.ai / cocapn.com — "Customize and manage."
- **Layer 3 — Build:** deckboss.ai / deckboss.net / capitaine.ai — "Design systems, open the hood."
